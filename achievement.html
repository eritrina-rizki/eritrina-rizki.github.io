Our Achievement

In the past year, 95% of our graduating students were accepted to top-ranked universities around the world, including Harvard, Oxford, and Stanford.

Our school received recognition as the top-performing high school in the state for the second year in a row, based on national and international exam scores.

Our teachers published several research papers and articles in prestigious academic journals, furthering the knowledge and understanding in their respective fields.

Our debate team won first place in the National High School Debate Championship, competing against schools from across the country.

Our school's music program organized a charity concert that raised over $10,000 for a local non-profit organization.

Our sports teams won several regional and state championships, including the state basketball championship and the regional soccer championship.

Our students volunteered over 5,000 hours of community service in the past year, supporting local charities and organizations focused on environmental conservation, education, and health care.

Our school established a new partnership with an international organization to support educational programs in developing countries, providing our students with valuable opportunities to engage in global service projects.

Our school's social responsibility initiatives received recognition from the United Nations, earning us the "Global School of the Year" award.

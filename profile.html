Our Profile

"Expand Your Horizons, Embrace Your Future"

About Horizon

Horizon, signifies the endless possibilities and opportunities that await our students in their journey towards a bright future. Just as the horizon extends infinitely beyond our sight, so too does the potential of our students. We strive to provide them with a supportive and challenging environment that helps them develop into well-rounded individuals with a strong sense of social responsibility and a commitment to lifelong learning.

We believe that education is a lifelong journey that starts with a strong foundation in high school. Horizon International High School is dedicated to providing a comprehensive education that prepares students for success in all aspects of life. Our faculty and staff are passionate about inspiring students to reach their full potential. We offer a wide range of courses and extracurricular activities that encourage creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration.

By choosing Horizon International High School, students are embarking on a journey that will broaden their perspectives, expand their horizons, and equip them with the tools they need to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To empower and inspire students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and responsible citizens who make a positive impact in their communities and the world.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive and rigorous education that fosters intellectual curiosity, creativity, and collaboration, and prepares students for success in college, the workforce, and beyond. We achieve this mission by:

  1. Offering a diverse range of academic programs and extracurricular activities that challenge and inspire students to achieve their full potential.
  2. Providing a safe and inclusive learning environment that supports the academic, social, and emotional growth of every student.
  3. Cultivating a culture of academic excellence, integrity, and service to others.
  4. Partnering with families and the community to promote student success and positive outcomes.
